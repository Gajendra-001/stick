Key Features

Our Smart Blind Stick combines cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design to provide comprehensive mobility assistance.

Obstacle Detection

Advanced ultrasonic and infrared sensors detect obstacles at ground level, head level, and identify potholes and vehicles in real-time.

  • • Ground-level obstacle detection
  • • Head-level hazard identification
  • • Pothole and vehicle detection
  • • Real-time audio feedback

GPS Tracking

Precise location tracking with geofencing capabilities, route recording, and location history for enhanced safety and navigation.

  • • Real-time location tracking
  • • Geofencing for safe zones
  • • Route recording and analysis
  • • Location history timeline

SOS Alerts

Emergency SOS system with instant alerts to guardians, emergency contacts, and emergency services with precise location information.

  • • One-touch SOS activation
  • • Instant guardian notifications
  • • Emergency services integration
  • • Location sharing
